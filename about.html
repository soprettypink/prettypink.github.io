About


I used an app called ToonMe to create my self portrait.


After graduating from Maryville University with a Master’s degree in Software Development, I’m currently pursuing a career in Web Design. I have a solid understanding of topography, user interface design, and other concepts within web design. Designing and developing websites and web apps on the front end so they show amazing interfaces and provide great user experience for users was a career I had dreamed of pursuing since I was an adolescent. I’ve gained more skills and knowledge in user interface design and user experience design for websites and then taken those designs to develop them on the front end. This will aid me when I go further in a serious career in the Technology industry in the future. Living and working overseas for several years has helped me gain new ideas and perspectives and I’d like a chance to share them. I understand various concepts of user interface and user experience design such as knowing the importance of recognizing user goals, conducting user research, understanding topography, building sitemaps, and storyboards, sketching low-fidelity wireframes, designing high-fidelity wireframes, and creating interactive prototypes. I’ve used Figma and Canva when creating logos and building high-fidelity wireframes and have some familiarity with Photoshop and Adobe XD. When it comes to Frontend Development, I have tested the websites that I’ve created as a project on various browsers and devices. I’m familiar with SASS, LESS, and Bootstrap. Please feel free to view my online portfolio that I’ve designed and built myself. The designs that I have on my online portfolio were created using Figma and Canva.
